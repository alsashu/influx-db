How to Use
  • Draw Track (Freehand):
    1. Click the "Draw Track" button (pencil icon turns blue)
    2. Click and drag on the canvas to draw your track path freehand
    3. Release the mouse to finish the current track
    4. The track automatically snaps to grid intersections for precision
    5. Draw multiple tracks by clicking and dragging again
  • Grid Size: Change the grid size (meters per square) to adjust measurement scale. All track lengths update automatically.
  • Select & Move: Click "Select" button, then click and drag tracks to reposition them.
  • Delete: In Select mode, click a track to select it, then click Delete button or press Delete key.
  • Track Length: Each track displays its length in meters as a label.
  • Export: Save your design as SVG (vector) or PNG (raster) image.
