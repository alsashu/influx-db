How to Use
  • Draw Track: Click "Draw Track" button, then click points on the grid. Each track gets a unique ID (1, 2, 3...). Double-click or press Enter to finish.
  • Track Extremities: Each track automatically has extremities at both ends (default: Connection type).
  • Change Extremity Type: Right-click on the extremity circles at track ends to change between Open End, Connection, or Buffer Stop.
  • Extend Tracks: You can only extend tracks from Open End extremities. Buffer Stops prevent extension.
  • Track IDs: Each track shows its numeric ID in the middle of the track.
  • Export railML: Export your design in railML 3.x format for professional railway systems.
